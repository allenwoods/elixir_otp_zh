# Part 1 Getting Started with Elixir & OTP


We begin with the basics of Elixir. We will answer some existential questions like why do we need Elixir and what is it good for. Then, we'll dive straight into a series of examples that demonstrate the various language features.


We then look at processes, the fundamental unit of concurrency in Elixir. We will see how processes in Elixir relate to the Actor concurrency model. If you have struggled with concurrency coming from other languages, Elixir will be a breath of fresh air.


We'll conclude part one with an introduction to OTP, and learn about the GenServer behaviour, the most basic and yet most important of all the behaviours.
